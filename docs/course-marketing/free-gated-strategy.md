| Tier | Content | Rationale |

## The Hot-Buyer Insight
The single most buyer-productive free asset is Lesson 2.1 ending with an honest map of everything still ahead — titles, FAQ schema, DNS, GSC, ongoing updates. **The wow plus the wall, in one lesson. Don't hide the wall; the wall is the salesman.**

## Urgency Aha — Per Module
1. "I never needed a developer — I needed to know this was possible."
2. "AI built a real site in an hour... but making it *mine* took my whole Sunday."
3. "My pages were invisible because my titles say what I do, not what customers search."
4. "When ChatGPT answers instead of Google, my site isn't even in the room."
5. "DNS records, Search Console, domain renewal — this isn't a weekend, it's a part-time job."
6. "The site is never done. Who's going to feed it every week — me?"

Modules 5–6 are where "I could do this" flips to "I don't *want* to do this." **That flip is the product.**
